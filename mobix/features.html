Features list

  • Photo gallery

    Easy create your own photo gallery app. List and full screen options available.

  • Blog layout

    Create your news app with blog and comments section.

  • User login

    Add a login form to your app to control your private content.

  • User account

    Create an user account page with profile image and custom menu.

  • Responsive tabs

    Add responsive tabs to any page or section in your app.

  • Toogles

    Add content with toogles will save a lot of space in your app layout.

  • Tables

    Create a table section for your services and price plans.

  • Images slider

    Create an image slider from your photos.

  • Contact form

    Receive messages from your clients using the allready build in contact form.

  • Custom form inputs

    Build custom forms with inputs, selects, radio, checkboxes.

  • Social share

    Allow users to follow you on your social websites.

  • Right slide panel

    Option to add a right slide panel with menu or other content.

  • External links

    To add an external link just use class="external" to the link code.
